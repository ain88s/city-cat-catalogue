Who We Are

City Cat Collectibles is a veteran-owned and operated business based in San Diego, California. We specialize in wholesale collectibles — trading cards, stickers, albums, and more — sourcing hard-to-find inventory for resellers, shops, and passionate collectors worldwide.

Our Focus

We operate exclusively in the wholesale space, offering bulk quantities at competitive prices. Whether you are stocking a store, building a personal collection, or flipping for profit, our catalogue is built with you in mind.
